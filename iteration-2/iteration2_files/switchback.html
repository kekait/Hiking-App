Switchback Trail Finder






Welcome to Switchback Trail Finder!

On the left-hand side of the menu bar is
the trail button. This will allow you to
select a trail to view on our map. To get
more information on a certain trail, click
on the pin at the start of that trail. Each
trail pin will display the name of the trail,
a short description, the trail length in
miles, the sunrise and sunset times, the
weather, and the wind speed.
